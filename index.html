Overview

In the United States, the pursuit of the American Dream has historically been synonymous with the idea of prosperity, financial security, and upward mobility. However, our project, "The Cost of Living: Struggling to Make Ends Meet," unveils a sobering reality: the dream of achieving a comfortable life is slipping further from the grasp of average income earners. This visual essay aims to illuminate the growing lack of opportunities for those who fall within the middle-income bracket, while also distinguishing between average, above-average, and below-average incomes per state.
